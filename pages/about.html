About Me

This bit will be a showcase of my setup. I have a 'micro home-lab' on the network with an array of Raspberry Pis which are organised as a NAS server, a web server and one for me to program and play about on, learning and familiarising myself with the Linux environment.


My daily drive is my PC which is Windows 11. A modest setup:

    Motherboard: AsRock
    CPU: AMD 5500 Series 3.6GHz
    Memory: 24 GB Corsair 2400Mhz
    Graphics Card: AMD RX570 8GB
    Solid State: 1.5TB of SSDs including a Samsung 980
    Hard Drive: 2TB Standard 7200rpm drive

This is built myself into a Corsair case with Air cooling and RGB with Corsair's iCue for a bit of flair. I am painfully aware that this is a slow system in today's market. Socket AM4 is 8 years old as of 2024 and I could upgrade the memory speed and the graphics card, I am working on it.

This system does run Cyberpunk, so I am happy with it and with AMD and nVidia's own AI processing speeding things up, gave the system more longetivity. It is a 5 year old system now.
