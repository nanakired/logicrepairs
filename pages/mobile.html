Mobile device servies

Having the basic tools and knowledge, there isn't a phone I can't get into. I have experience with Samsung models but, sadly, I do not work on Apple products, because of the expense of their tools and such as the 'closed' nature of their business. Maybe on older Apple phones (circa 2015).

Please note: if you still have guarantees, warranties or some such plan on your device, then contact the manufacturer or place of sale in question.

Mobile Phones

Broken Screens

Let me know of the model of the phone and I can order a part in with a warranty and fit the part

Charger Ports

This can be a few things from fluff and dust having built up inside the port right up to a clean break of the cable, let me know and I can fix these

Sound Issues

Microphone and speaker ports can be replaced in most devices, again, seperate from the main boards, can also be a cheaper option.



Tablets

Broken Screens

Tablets do tend to be quite expensive for screens as such, it is cheaper to buy a replacement device second hand or even new on sale. Regardless, much like mobile phones, once I know the model and part needed I have the tools and experience.

Charger Ports

These are a lot easier to replace in some cases being a serperate small power PCB board in the device. I can also solder and replace USB ports if needed.
